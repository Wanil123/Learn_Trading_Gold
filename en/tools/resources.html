🔗 Resources

Sites, books, YouTube channels — only legitimate sources. Zero paid gurus.

📅 Economic calendars & news (check BEFORE each trade)

  • ForexFactory Calendar — the free reference, filters by impact
  • Investing.com — consensus + revisions, very comprehensive
  • Myfxbook Calendar — + retail sentiment (useful for contrarian analysis)
  • DailyFX Calendar — analysis (read with caution)

🥇 Gold-specific / macro

  • Kitco — real-time spot price, gold sector news
  • World Gold Council — quarterly Gold Demand Trends reports, central bank data
  • FRED (Fed St. Louis) — real rates (DFII10), DXY, inflation
  • CFTC COT Report — positioning of major players (GC futures), published Fridays
  • Reuters Commodities — factual news

📚 Essential books

  • Mark Douglas — Trading in the Zone — psychology, the bible
  • Brett Steenbarger — The Daily Trading Coach — 101 practical lessons
  • Van Tharp — Trade Your Way to Financial Freedom — position sizing, R-multiples
  • Alexander Elder — Trading for a Living — Mind, Method, Money
  • Jack Schwager — Market Wizards (series) — interviews with professional traders
  • Adam Grimes — The Art and Science of Technical Analysis — statistical rigor
  • Bob Volman — Forex Price Action Scalping — explicit, backtestable rules
  • Al Brooks — Reading Price Charts Bar by Bar — pure price action

🛠️ Free tools

  • TradingView — charts, bar replay for manual backtesting
  • MT4/MT5 — free Strategy Tester, backtests on historical data
  • Forex Tester 5 Lite — free demo version
  • Myfxbook — demo/live account tracking, automatic stats

⚠️ TO AVOID

Common scams
  • "Mentors" on Instagram/TikTok/Telegram selling courses for $500-$3000
  • Paid signal sellers
  • Prop firms selling their challenges as "education"
  • Any promise of fixed returns or a "100% winrate strategy"
  • "Magic" bots/EAs sold on MQL5 or Telegram
  • Courses that never mention realistic losses and drawdown

📖 Official studies to read once

  • ESMA — 74-89% of retail CFD accounts lose money
  • AMF France — 89% losers over 4 years, average loss of -10,887 EUR
  • Barber & Odean — "Trading is Hazardous to Your Wealth" (<1% of day traders profitable long term)
